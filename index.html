OUR GOALS


WE LISTEN

The first step in our four step process requires that we listen to your questions. We wish it didn't require that. Seriously, we created these steps and regret even including any of the first three.

WE CONFER

The sign of quality support is the speed and acuracy of a valuable solution. This isn't that. Remember, this is Not Nice Support. Our goal is to make it look as if your problem is the hardest issue facing our team of experts. It's not of course, but you don't know that, and the longer we take to answer your question the more we can legally charge.

WE CREATE

This is one of the hardest things we do second only to that stupid listening bullshit thing in step one. Sure, we have some overly simplistic answers like "That's your problem" or "Let me Google that for you" but the pleasure experienced in finding a new Not Nice Support Retort™ is why we put up with you in the first place. (Well that plus step four.)

WE CHARGE

In retrospect we probably should have started with a policy of up front charges. Honestly we're exhausted by steps one through three. This is the step that keeps the alcohol budget down. Who are we kidding? This is the step that pays for the alcohol.
